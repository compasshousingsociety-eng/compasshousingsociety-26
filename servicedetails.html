Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Property Search service

How many properties will I see with this service? +

Our basic package includes review of 25+ properties and physical viewings of 10 selected properties. Premium packages offer unlimited property reviews and viewings until you find your perfect match.

What areas do you cover? +

We cover all major metropolitan areas and suburbs nationwide. Our network extends to 50+ cities with local experts in each region to provide you with the best local knowledge and access to exclusive listings.

How long does the property search process take? +

The standard service duration is 60 days, but most clients find their ideal property within 30-45 days. We work according to your timeline and can expedite the search process if needed.

Do you offer virtual tours? +

Yes, we provide high-quality virtual tours for all shortlisted properties. This allows you to narrow down your choices before scheduling physical visits, saving you time and travel expenses.

What happens if I don't find a property during the service period? +

If you don't find a suitable property during the initial service period, we offer a 50% discount on extending the service. Our goal is to ensure you find your perfect home, and we continue working with you until you do.
